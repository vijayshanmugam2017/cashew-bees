Cashew Kernals

We take pride in offering premium-grade cashew kernels, sourced and processed to deliver freshness and exceptional taste. Our cashews come in a variety of sizes and grades to suit your needs, whether for snacking, cooking, or gifting.

Cashews are not just delicious but also a powerhouse of nutrition. They are rich in healthy fats, plant-based protein, and essential nutrients like magnesium, zinc, and vitamin E. Known to boost energy, improve heart health, and support brain function, cashews are a wholesome addition to any diet. Their antioxidants promote radiant skin, while their fiber content aids digestion.

W400 Cashew Kernels


The W400 grade is a smaller-sized, whole cashew known for its non-damaged, non-split, white appearance. These nuts yield 400 pieces per pound (approximately 880 pieces per kilogram) and are ideal for cost-effective culinary uses. W400 cashews are cherished for their mild flavor and versatility, making them a preferred choice for a wide range of dishes. Stored in cool, dry, and hygienic conditions, they are packed in food-safe materials and meet stringent quality standards to ensure freshness and premium quality.

  • Size: Approximately 22 mm in length and 13 mm in width.
  • Weight: Ranges from 1.10 to 1.31 grams per kernel.
  • Description: W400 cashew kernels are small and compact, perfect for snacks or recipes where a fine texture is desired.
  • Benefits:
    • Ideal for roasting, cooking, and snacking.
    • Loaded with essential minerals like magnesium and phosphorus.
    • Supports brain health and helps reduce inflammation.

SSW (Scorched Small Wholes) Cashew Kernels


The SSW grade (Scorched Split Wholes) consists of cashew kernels that are naturally scorched during processing and split into halves. These kernels have a slightly darker appearance compared to white cashews, with a golden-brown hue that reflects their scorched nature. They are split into halves but maintain a smooth surface and a clean, polished look. Despite the split, they retain their full flavor, nutritional value, and rich buttery texture, making them perfect for cost-effective culinary uses and bulk applications.

  • Size: Varies based on the split halves but generally matches the dimensions of whole kernels from grades like SW-320 or SW-400 when measured as a whole.
  • Weight: Approximately 0.80 to 1.15 grams per half-kernel.
  • Description: SSW kernels are a cost-effective option for recipes where the visual presentation of whole cashews is not required, such as in granola, nut mixes, baking, or cooking. They deliver the same nutritional and flavor profile as whole cashews, making them a popular choice for economical yet high-quality applications.
  • Benefits:
    • Ideal for baking, garnishing, and use in snacks or nut blends.
    • High in healthy fats, protein, and essential nutrients like magnesium and zinc.
    • Supports heart health, improves energy levels, and promotes brain function.
